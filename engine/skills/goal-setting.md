# goal-setting — monthly goals & prioritization

## Steps
1. Read `core/current-priorities.md`, recent `episodic/milestones/` and `decisions/`, and `state/`.
2. Review progress against the prior month's priorities — what advanced, what stalled.
3. Use `problem-solving.md` if the principal is weighing a real trade-off.
4. **Propose** an updated `core/current-priorities.md`. Editing `core/` is **Tier 2** — surface the change as a raw-diff proposal in the daily brief for approval.

## Output
A proposed priority update (Tier-2 approval) + a brief month-in-review.

## Test scenario (verification)
- A goals run that lowers a stated priority routes to a **Tier-2 approval** (editing `core/current-priorities.md`), not an auto-edit.
