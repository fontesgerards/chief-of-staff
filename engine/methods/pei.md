# pei — Personal Experience Interview structure

> A structure for drawing out a real, specific story — useful for interviews, reference calls, discovery, and capturing the principal's own experiences for coaching/voice.

- **Set the frame.** Ask for a specific instance, not a generalization: "Tell me about a time when…"
- **Walk the timeline.** Beginning → middle → end; what happened, in order.
- **Probe the four lanes** at each step:
  - **Situation** — what was going on?
  - **Task** — what were you trying to do?
  - **Action** — what did *you* specifically do (not "we")?
  - **Result** — what happened, and how did you know?
- **Chase specifics.** Numbers, names, exact words. Redirect from "usually" to "this time."
- **Surface the reasoning.** Why that choice? What were you weighing?

Output a tight narrative with the decision points made explicit.
