# strengths-coaching — strengths-based leadership development

> Used by the weekly `coaching` skill. Coach from strengths, not deficits.

- **Anchor on strengths.** Identify what the principal does distinctively well and how to apply it to the current challenge.
- **Role-relevant.** Frame development against the actual demands of their role right now (read `core/identity.md` + `core/current-priorities.md`).
- **Specific over general.** "In the Plata call you led with product before their pain" beats "work on discovery."
- **One or two moves, not a list.** Development sticks when it's focused.
- **Evidence-based.** Ground observations in real episodes (`memory/episodic/`), not impressions.
- **Forward-looking.** End with a concrete experiment for the coming week.
